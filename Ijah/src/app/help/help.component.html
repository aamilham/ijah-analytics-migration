Help / FAQ

Ijah is used for:
  • Given plants/compounds and proteins/diseases, you want to know the connectivity from plants to compounds to proteins to diseases.
  • Given plants/compounds, you want to know targeted proteins/diseases.
  • Given proteins/diseases, you want to know plants/compounds that target (potentially) cure such proteins/diseases.
  • Validating/cross-checking whether some plant compositions, i.e., Jamu formulas, have certain efficacy.
