About IJAH Analytics

IJAH is an advanced platform designed to explore the intricate relationships between plants, compounds, proteins, and diseases. It enables researchers and practitioners to trace connections from natural sources to their potential therapeutic applications. Whether you're investigating the connectivity from plants to compounds and their impact on specific proteins and diseases, or seeking to identify which plants or compounds target certain health conditions, IJAH provides the insights you need.

Additionally, the platform offers a robust system for validating traditional herbal compositions, such as Jamu formulas, by analyzing their efficacy through scientific data. By blending the wisdom of natural remedies with the precision of modern research, IJAH accelerates the discovery of plant-based therapies and promotes innovation in natural medicine.

PUI-PT TropBRC

IJAH Analytics operates under the auspices of the Center of Excellence for Tropical Biodiversity Research and Conservation (PUI-PT TropBRC) at IPB University.
